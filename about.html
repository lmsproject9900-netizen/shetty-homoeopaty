ABOUT OUR INSTITUTE

  • 01 Shetty Homoeopathic medical College and Hospital is a unit of Shetty Group of Institutions, (SGI) founded by Mr. Uday Shankar Shetty, a dynamic personality and the mastermind behind this prestigious organization. Shetty Group of Institutions established in the year 2000.
  • 02 Shetty Group of Institutions comprises of Engineering, Homoeopathy, Ayurvedic, Pharmacy, Nursing and Paramedical institutions. This education major has set a hall mark standard in professional education sector which allows it to redefine its own standards consistently.
  • 03 The 20 acres campus is replete with facilities like spacious classrooms, smart classrooms, well equipped labs, hostel, food court, Playground etc. Located on Shahabad road, Kalaburagi, Karnataka. SGI has dedicated itself to the cause of providing quality education.
  • 04 Excellence that is reflected in the innovative conduct and advancement of education, research and patient care.
  • 05 Respect for individuals who are affiliated with or come in contact with Shetty Homeopathy Medical College & Hospital: staff, students, residents, fellows, faculty, staff, partners, communities, patients and families.
  • 06 Integrity that embraces the very highest standards of ethical behavior and exemplary moral character. Diversity that is reflected in actions that appreciate all individuals.
