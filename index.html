About Shetty Sangappa Memorial Trust

Empowering young minds with technical & entrepreneurial knowledge since 2011

Shetty Group of Institutions is a part of Shetty Sangappa Memorial Trust established in 2011 to empower young budding brains with technical and entrepreneurial knowledge and skills, assisting them in holistic growth. Located just on the other side of the Highway (NH 150) on Shahbad road, Kalaburagi, the college nestles amidst sprawling 17 acres of greenery with a hilly backdrop.

The campus breathes fresh air of nature with vast greenery, free from pollution. The ambience is ideal for learning, research, living and exploration. Shetty Homoeopathic Medical College and Hospital is a flagship unit of SGI, which comprises Engineering, Homoeopathy, Ayurvedic, Pharmacy, Nursing, and Paramedical institutions.

This education major has set a hallmark standard in professional education — redefining its own standards consistently.
